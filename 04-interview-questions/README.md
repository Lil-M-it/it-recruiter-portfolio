# Interview Questions by Role & Seniority

This section contains interview questions I commonly use to assess candidates across different IT roles and seniority levels.

---

## Junior Software Engineer

- Tell me about a project you worked on recently.
- What programming languages do you use most?
- How do you debug your code?
- How do you continue learning new technologies?

---

## Mid-Level Software Engineer

- Describe a technical challenge you solved.
- How do you ensure code quality?
- Have you worked with APIs or microservices?
- How do you collaborate with product or QA teams?

---

## Senior Software Engineer

- How do you design scalable systems?
- How do you make technical decisions?
- Have you mentored junior engineers?
- How do you balance speed vs quality?

---

## DevOps Engineer

- Which CI/CD tools have you used?
- How do you manage infrastructure as code?
- How do you monitor and ensure system reliability?
- Describe an incident you handled in production.

---

## Product Manager (Tech)

- How do you define product success?
- How do you prioritize features?
- How do you work with engineering teams?
- How do you translate business needs into technical requirements?
