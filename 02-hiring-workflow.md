# Recruitment Process

This section describes my end-to-end recruitment methodology, used in consultancy and in-house environments.

## 1. Intake Meeting with Hiring Manager
- Understand business context and team structure
- Clarify technical requirements (must-have / nice-to-have)
- Define seniority level, budget range, and hiring timeline
- Align on interview stages and decision-makers

## 2. Sourcing Strategy
- Boolean search (LinkedIn, GitHub, job boards)
- Market mapping and talent pipeline creation
- Passive candidate outreach
- Employer branding aligned messaging

## 3. Screening & Evaluation
- CV screening and shortlisting
- HR interview (motivation, soft skills, culture fit)
- Technical pre-screening (role-dependent)
- Salary expectations and availability check

## 4. Interview Coordination
- Scheduling interviews with stakeholders
- Candidate preparation and briefing
- Feedback collection and alignment

## 5. Offer & Closing
- Offer presentation and negotiation
- Counteroffer management
- Candidate experience follow-up
- Onboarding handover
