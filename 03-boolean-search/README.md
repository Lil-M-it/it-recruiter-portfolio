# Boolean Search Strategies for IT Recruitment

This section contains Boolean search examples I use to source IT and tech profiles across different seniority levels.

---

## Backend Engineer

("Backend Developer" OR "Backend Engineer" OR "Software Engineer")
AND (Java OR Python OR Node OR ".NET")
AND (API OR Microservices)
AND (SQL OR NoSQL)

---

## Frontend Developer

("Frontend Developer" OR "Frontend Engineer")
AND (JavaScript OR TypeScript)
AND (React OR Angular OR Vue)

---

## DevOps Engineer

("DevOps Engineer" OR "Site Reliability Engineer" OR SRE)
AND (AWS OR Azure OR GCP)
AND (Docker OR Kubernetes OR CI/CD)

---

## Product Manager (Tech)

("Product Manager" OR "Technical Product Manager")
AND (SaaS OR "Software Product")
AND (Agile OR Scrum)
