# Full-Cycle IT Recruitment Process

This document outlines my end-to-end recruitment process, from initial intake to successful hiring and onboarding.

---

## 1. Intake Meeting with Hiring Manager
- Understand business context, team structure, and project needs
- Clarify technical requirements, must-haves, and nice-to-haves
- Define seniority level, budget range, and timeline
- Align on interview stages and decision-makers

---

## 2. Role Definition & Market Analysis
- Create a clear and realistic job profile
- Analyze talent availability and market competitiveness
- Advise hiring managers on salary benchmarks and expectations
- Adjust requirements based on market insights when needed

---

## 3. Sourcing Strategy
- Define target companies and candidate personas
- Use Boolean search (LinkedIn, GitHub, job boards)
- Leverage direct sourcing, referrals, and internal databases
- Prioritize diversity and quality of profiles

---

## 4. Candidate Screening
- CV and profile review
- Initial recruiter interview (motivation, experience, cultural fit)
- Technical background validation aligned with the role
- Clear communication about process and expectations

---

## 5. Interview Process Management
- Coordinate interviews with stakeholders
- Prepare candidates for each interview stage
- Collect structured feedback from interviewers
- Act as main point of contact between candidates and company

---

## 6. Offer Management
- Support salary negotiations
- Align candidate expectations with company constraints
- Manage counteroffers and closing strategies
- Ensure a positive candidate experience

---

## 7. Onboarding & Follow-Up
- Support candidate during onboarding phase
- Follow up after start date to ensure successful integration
- Maintain long-term relationship with hired candidates
